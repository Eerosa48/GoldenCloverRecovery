Functional Massage

A specialized therapeutic approach that integrates various massage techniques to enhance the body's functional performance, mobility, and overall well-being. This modality focuses on addressing specific musculoskeletal issues, improving joint range of motion, and optimizing the body's natural movement patterns.

Benefits

  • Improved mobility
  • Pain relief
  • Enhanced athletic performance
  • Stress reduction
  • Postural correction

What to Expect

During a functional massage session, the therapist will assess your specific needs and tailor the treatment accordingly. Techniques may include myofascial release, trigger point therapy, deep tissue massage, and stretching. The goal is to not only relieve pain and tension but also to enhance the body's functional capabilities, making it ideal for those recovering from injury, athletes, or anyone looking to improve their physical health and movement efficiency.


Cupping Therapy

A technique used during functional massage in order to enhance blood flow, reduce muscle tension, promote healing, and decompress the fascia.

Benefits

  • Enhanced circulation
  • Muscle relaxation
  • Pain relief
  • Myofascial decompression
  • Improved mobility

What to Expect

During a session, cups are placed on specific areas of the body where fascial tightness is identified. The cups create suction, lifting the skin and fascia. Clients might feel a pulling sensation, and circular marks may appear, which are temporary and typically fade within a few days.


Gua-sha (Scraping)

A traditional East Asian technique that uses a smooth-edged tool to scrape the skin, enhancing circulation, reducing muscle tightness, and promoting healing.

Benefits

  • Improved circulation
  • Pain relief
  • Promotes relaxation and healing

What to Expect

During a Gua Sha session, the practitioner scrapes the skin with a smooth-edged tool, causing temporary redness or bruising that fades within a few days. Clients typically experience immediate relief from tension and pain, along with a sense of relaxation and improved well-being.


IV Hydration Therapy

A convenient and quick way to receive fluids, electrolytes, and nutrients. It can be used for basic hydration, athletic recovery, illness, and/or hangover.

Benefits

  • Rapid rehydration
  • Enhanced energy
  • Improved recovery
  • Boosted immunity
  • Glowing skin
  • Mental clarity

What to Expect

During your IV hydration session, you’ll start with a consultation to discuss your health goals and medical history. A personalized IV formula will be used to meet your specific needs. The administration process is comfortable and typically takes 30–60 minutes. You’ll feel the benefits quickly, experiencing enhanced hydration and increased energy.
