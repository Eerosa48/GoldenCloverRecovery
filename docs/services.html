Functional Massage

Book Now

Our functional massage blends therapeutic bodywork with movement, cupping, and scraping to relieve pain, improve mobility, and restore balance. Each session is tailored to your body’s needs, addressing areas of tension or dysfunction to support long-term recovery, performance, and overall well-being.


Benefits

  • Improved mobility
  • Pain relief
  • Enhanced athletic performance
  • Stress reduction
  • Postural correction

What to Expect

During a functional massage session, the therapist will assess your specific needs and tailor the treatment accordingly. Techniques may include myofascial release, trigger point therapy, deep tissue massage, and stretching. The goal is to not only relieve pain and tension but also to enhance the body's functional capabilities, making it ideal for those recovering from injury, athletes, or anyone looking to improve their physical health and movement efficiency.


Prenatal / Postpartum Massage

Book Now

Our functional prenatal and postpartum massage supports the body through pregnancy and postpartum using safe, therapeutic, and movement-informed bodywork. Each session is tailored to your stage of pregnancy or postpartum recovery, addressing common areas of discomfort while supporting alignment, circulation, and overall well-being.

Using a specialized pregnancy pillow system, we are able to safely position you face-down, allowing for more effective treatment of the hips, low back, shoulders, and other areas impacted by pregnancy and postpartum changes.


Benefits

  • Relief from low back, hip, and pelvic tension
  • Improved circulation and reduced swelling
  • Support for postural changes during pregnancy
  • Decreased muscle tension in the neck and shoulders
  • Enhanced recovery and body awareness postpartum

What to Expect

During a prenatal or postpartum functional massage session, the therapist will assess your individual needs and stage of pregnancy or recovery. Techniques may include gentle myofascial release, targeted therapeutic massage, cupping (when appropriate), stretching, and supportive positioning.

The goal is to relieve discomfort, support your changing body, and promote recovery—making this ideal for expectant mothers and those navigating postpartum healing.


IV Hydration Therapy

Book Now

IV Hydration Therapy is a fast, effective way to replenish fluids, electrolytes, and essential nutrients. Whether you’re looking for basic hydration, athletic recovery, immune support, or relief from illness or fatigue, this therapy helps your body recover and perform at its best.


Benefits

  • Rapid rehydration
  • Boosted energy and mental clarity
  • Enhanced recovery and performance
  • Immune system support
  • Improved skin hydration and glow

What to Expect

Your session begins with a brief consultation to review your health goals and medical history. A personalized IV formula is selected to meet your individual needs and administered in a comfortable, relaxing setting. The process typically takes 60 minutes, and many clients notice the benefits quickly, leaving feeling refreshed, recharged, and revitalized.
