Functional Massage Book Now

Our functional massage blends therapeutic bodywork with movement, cupping, and scraping to relieve pain, improve mobility, and restore balance. Each session is tailored to your body’s needs, addressing areas of tension or dysfunction to support long-term recovery, performance, and overall well-being.

Benefits

  • Improved mobility
  • Pain relief
  • Enhanced athletic performance
  • Stress reduction
  • Postural correction

What to Expect

During a functional massage session, the therapist will assess your specific needs and tailor the treatment accordingly. Techniques may include myofascial release, trigger point therapy, deep tissue massage, and stretching. The goal is to not only relieve pain and tension but also to enhance the body's functional capabilities, making it ideal for those recovering from injury, athletes, or anyone looking to improve their physical health and movement efficiency.


Prenatal / Postpartum Massage Book Now

Our functional prenatal / postpartum massage supports the body through pregnancy and postpartum with targeted, therapeutic work. Using a specially designed pregnancy pillow system, we can safely position you face-down to address areas like the hips, low back, and shoulders more effectively. This allows for greater relief, improved circulation, and better alignment as your body changes. Each session is customized for your stage of pregnancy and/or postpartum.

What to Expect

During your IV hydration session, you’ll start with a consultation to discuss your health goals and medical history. A personalized IV formula will be used to meet your specific needs.
