ABOUT

My name is Danielle Erosa, I am a Massage Therapist and Paramedic with over a decade of experience in the medical field. My journey has given me a deep understanding of anatomy and physiology, fueling my passion for helping people move and feel their best through manual therapy via functional massage, movement, and IV hydration. On my days off you can find me hiking, hanging out with my husband and pups, or on the patio with a good cup of coffee.

I would love to connect and discover how I can support your wellness journey! Reach out to make an appointment today to get you feeling your best.
