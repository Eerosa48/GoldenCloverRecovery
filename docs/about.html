ABOUT

My name is Danielle Erosa, and I am a Massage Therapist, Paramedic, and Mom with over a decade of experience in the medical field. My journey has given me a deep understanding of anatomy and physiology, fueling my passion for helping people move and feel their best through functional massage, movement, IV hydration, and specialized prenatal and postpartum care.

On my days off, you can find me hiking, spending time with my husband, son, and pups, or relaxing on the patio with a good cup of coffee.

I would love to connect and discover how I can support your wellness journey. Reach out to make an appointment today and get you feeling your best.

The Family and Inspiration

My husband has been my biggest supporter from day one, cheering me on through every step of building this business. Becoming a mom and experiencing pregnancy firsthand completely changed my perspective, sparking my passion for prenatal and postpartum care. I know how demanding this season can be, and I’m here to support women with care that’s informed, intuitive, and supportive.

Golden Clover was inspired by our pups—Ellie(left), Clover(middle), and Atlas(right). They are at the heart of the name, which reflects what I believe in most: strong women, resilience, and showing up bravely through every season.
